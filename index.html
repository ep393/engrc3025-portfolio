About Me

Hi, I am Emir.

My job consists in helping companies and researchers to analyse and visualize their datasets. I am skilled for most data-science steps: data pre-processing, application of statistical methods, data visualization and results communication.

I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Cornell University. I have a passion for turning complex data into compelling stories that are both insightful and easy to understand. With experience in various programming languages like Python and R, and tools like Tableau, Power BI, and D3.js, I am equipped to tackle diverse data challenges. My projects range from interactive dashboards for business intelligence to intricate data visualizations for academic research, each tailored to the unique needs and goals of my clients.
